**Fitness Plan for 27-Year-Old Male (5.6 - 5.7 feet, 180 pounds)**

**Objective:** Gain muscle and lose fat.

**Duration:** 12 weeks

**Weekly Schedule:**

- **Frequency:** 5 days per week
- **Rest Days:** 2 days (e.g., Wednesday and Sunday)

### Workout Plan

**Day 1: Upper Body Strength**
- **Warm-up (10 minutes):** Dynamic stretches (arm circles, torso twists)
- **Bench Press:** 3 sets of 8-10 reps
- **Dumbbell Rows:** 3 sets of 8-10 reps (each arm)
- **Overhead Shoulder Press:** 3 sets of 8-10 reps
- **Bicep Curls:** 3 sets of 10 reps
- **Tricep Dips:** 3 sets to failure
- **Cool Down (5-10 minutes):** Stretching focusing on upper body

**Day 2: Lower Body Strength**
- **Warm-up (10 minutes):** Dynamic stretches (leg swings, hip circles)
- **Squats:** 3 sets of 8-10 reps
- **Deadlifts:** 3 sets of 8-10 reps
- **Leg Press:** 3 sets of 10 reps
- **Lunges:** 3 sets of 10 reps (each side)
- **Calf Raises:** 3 sets of 15 reps
- **Cool Down (5-10 minutes):** Stretching focusing on lower body

**Day 3: Cardio and Core**
- **Warm-up (10 minutes):** Light jogging or cycling
- **Cardio (30 minutes):** Alternate between HIIT (High-Intensity Interval Training) and steady-state cardio; Example: 20 seconds sprint, 40 seconds walk (repeat for 20 minutes)
- **Core Circuit (3 rounds):**
  - Plank: 30 seconds
  - Bicycle Crunches: 15 reps on each side
  - Russian Twists: 15 reps on each side
- **Cool Down (5-10 minutes):** Core and back stretching

**Day 4: Full Body Strength**
- **Warm-up (10 minutes):** Dynamic stretches
- **Barbell Squat to Press (Thrusters):** 3 sets of 8-10 reps
- **Pull-Ups or Assisted Pull-Ups:** 3 sets to failure
- **Push-Ups:** 3 sets of 10-15 reps
- **Kettlebell Swings:** 3 sets of 10 reps
- **Mountain Climbers:** 3 sets of 30 seconds
- **Cool Down (5-10 minutes):** Full body stretching

**Day 5: Active Recovery or Low-Intensity Cardio**
- **Activities:** Walking, yoga, cycling, or a light swim (30-45 minutes)
- **Focus on flexibility and recovery**

### Notes on Workout:
- Proper form is critical to prevent injuries; seek guidance if unsure.
- Gradually increase weights as strength improves.
- Stay hydrated throughout the workouts.

### Goals:
- Track progress weekly, aiming to increase weights or reps.
- Aim to work up to at least 150 minutes of moderate aerobic exercise each week.

**Nutrition Plan:**

To enhance muscle gain and fat loss, it's essential to consume nutrient-dense meals with an emphasis on protein, healthy fats, and carbohydrates. Below are meal options with recipes.

### Meal Plan

**Breakfast Options:**

1. **Protein-Packed Scramble**
   - **Ingredients:** 3 eggs, 1 cup spinach, 1/2 bell pepper, 1/4 onion, salt, pepper, and olive oil.
   - **Recipe:** Sauté chopped onion and bell pepper in olive oil. Add spinach until wilted. Scramble in eggs, season, and cook until done.
   - **Nutrition:** 300 calories, 20g protein, 18g fat, 12g carbs.

2. **Greek Yogurt Parfait**
   - **Ingredients:** 1 cup Greek yogurt, 1/2 cup mixed berries, 1/4 cup granola, honey (optional).
   - **Recipe:** Layer yogurt with berries and granola in a bowl. Drizzle with honey.
   - **Nutrition:** 350 calories, 20g protein, 10g fat, 40g carbs.

3. **Overnight Oats**
   - **Ingredients:** 1/2 cup rolled oats, 1 cup almond milk, 1 scoop protein powder, banana slices.
   - **Recipe:** Mix oats, milk, and protein in a jar. Top with banana. Refrigerate overnight.
   - **Nutrition:** 400 calories, 25g protein, 8g fat, 60g carbs.

**Lunch Options:**

1. **Quinoa Salad with Chicken**
   - **Ingredients:** 1 cup cooked quinoa, 4oz grilled chicken, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, 1/4 avocado, lemon juice, spices.
   - **Recipe:** Toss all ingredients in a bowl and dress with lemon juice.
   - **Nutrition:** 450 calories, 40g protein, 15g fat, 40g carbs.

2. **Turkey Wrap**
   - **Ingredients:** Whole grain wrap, 4oz turkey breast, spinach, hummus, sliced tomatoes.
   - **Recipe:** Spread hummus on the wrap, layer turkey, spinach, and tomatoes, then roll up.
   - **Nutrition:** 350 calories, 30g protein, 8g fat, 40g carbs.

3. **Buddha Bowl**
   - **Ingredients:** 1/2 cup brown rice, 1/2 cup chickpeas, mixed greens, diced bell pepper, tahini sauce.
   - **Recipe:** Layer rice, chickpeas, greens, and peppers. Drizzle with tahini sauce.
   - **Nutrition:** 500 calories, 18g protein, 15g fat, 70g carbs.

**Dinner Options:**

1. **Salmon with Asparagus**
   - **Ingredients:** 4oz salmon fillet, 1 cup asparagus, olive oil, lemon, spices.
   - **Recipe:** Bake salmon and asparagus drizzled with olive oil and spices.
   - **Nutrition:** 400 calories, 35g protein, 25g fat, 10g carbs.

2. **Stir-fried Tofu with Veggies**
   - **Ingredients:** 1 cup cubed tofu, mixed vegetables (broccoli, bell pepper, snap peas), soy sauce, ginger, garlic.
   - **Recipe:** Sauté garlic and ginger, add tofu and veggies, stir-fry until cooked, and add soy sauce.
   - **Nutrition:** 300 calories, 20g protein, 15g fat, 20g carbs.

3. **Beef and Vegetable Skewers**
   - **Ingredients:** 4oz lean beef, bell peppers, zucchini, onion, olive oil, spices.
   - **Recipe:** Skewer beef and veggies. Brush with olive oil and grill until cooked.
   - **Nutrition:** 400 calories, 35g protein, 20g fat, 10g carbs.

**Snacks Options:**

1. **Almonds & Fruit**
   - **Ingredients:** 1oz almonds, 1 apple.
   - **Nutrition:** 200 calories, 6g protein, 12g fat, 24g carbs.

2. **Cottage Cheese with Pineapple**
   - **Ingredients:** 1 cup low-fat cottage cheese, 1/2 cup pineapple.
   - **Nutrition:** 180 calories, 25g protein, 1g fat, 20g carbs.

3. **Protein Shake**
   - **Ingredients:** 1 scoop protein powder, 1 cup almond milk, 1 banana.
   - **Nutrition:** 300 calories, 25g protein, 5g fat, 40g carbs.

### Summary:

By following this structured workout plan alongside the diverse and delicious meal options provided, the individual should see significant progress toward their goals of gaining muscle and losing fat within the 12-week timeframe. Consistency and commitment to both diet and exercise will be crucial. Regular assessments and adjustments to the plan based on progress will ensure continued motivation and success. Remember, every step counts, and stay focused on your journey!
